Android Sensors Overview

In this page, I go over three useful sites to learn about mobile sensors and their capabilities. I have listed three websites that were useful to me , so I can reference back. I have also gave them a rating out of 5, to show how useful they are compared to the other sites I posted on here.

  1. YouTube -What Sensors Are in A Smartphone?

    In this YouTube Video, they go over the most important and most used sensors on your smartphone. The guy does a good job explaining what each sensor does and is used for. I found it very usefule when he breaks it down in categories. It helped me choose the right one for what kind ofnapp I wanted to create.

    Rating: 5 out of 5

  2. Mobile Cell Phone Repairing

    Information about newer and modern sensors that have now been imoplemented in newer generation smartphone. They go over the newest added sensors in a mobile device and how they are used. This one I find really helpful because they even include a video showing a diagram where the sensors are located on a smartphone.

    Rating: 5 out of 5

  3. Crime Studios Mobile Sensors and Data

    This site gives you information on modern new sensors and how they work on mobile devices. They also include the original sensors and how they are used. They explain how data is captured with sensors.

    Rating: 4 out of 5
